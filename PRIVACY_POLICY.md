# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 28, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

SmartWatt collects the following information:

### User Account Information

- Email address
- Full name (optional)
- Password (hashed and encrypted)

### Device & Energy Data

- Device information (name, category, wattage, usage hours)
- Energy consumption history (kWh per day)
- Budget settings
- Notification preferences

### Profile Information

- Type of residence (house, apartment, dormitory)
- Number of occupants
- Electrical power rating (VA)
- Tariff group
- Tariff per kWh

### Technical Information

- Device ID
- App version
- Operating system
- IP address (from API calls)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **To provide electricity monitoring services:** Track energy consumption and provide usage analytics
- **To generate recommendations:** AI-powered energy-saving recommendations via Google Gemini API
- **To calculate budgets:** Set and track monthly electricity budgets
- **To improve the app:** Fix bugs, optimize performance, and enhance features
- **To communicate:** Send notifications about energy usage and recommendations (if enabled)

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All user data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- User passwords are hashed with SHA-256 encryption
- API communication uses HTTPS encryption
- We do not store your data on our servers permanently
- No third-party analytics or tracking tools are used

## 4. Third-Party Services

SmartWatt uses:

- **Google Gemini API:** For AI-powered energy recommendations
  - Google processes your energy data to generate recommendations
  - Refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Edit or delete your profile information
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Disable notifications
- Opt-out of AI recommendations

## 6. Data Retention

- User data is retained as long as your account is active
- After account deletion, all data is permanently removed
- No backup copies are retained

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
